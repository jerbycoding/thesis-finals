### Analysis of Refined Shift 2 Anchors
*   **Search Optimization:** Every anchor word (e.g., `JSMITH`, `BROWSER PATCH`) now exists as a literal string in the `LogResource` message or source fields. This eliminates "zero-result" frustration.
*   **Narrative Flow:** The clues remain diegetic. For example, instead of saying "Search for VPN Gateway," the ticket mentions connections *originating* from the gateway, guiding the player to use that as their search filter.
*   **Difficulty Scaling:** Shift 2 still requires more investigation than Shift 1. In `VPN-ANOMALY-001`, the player must still figure out which of the two resulting logs is the "malicious" one by comparing the locations.
